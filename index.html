Welcome to JYM Engineering (S) Pte Ltd

Our purpose built factory has production facility which enable us to laser cut, punch, mould and weld sheets of stainless steel and a host of other metals of up to required thickness. We use the latest laser equipment which swiftly cut and lower operating costs. This means that we are able to pass the cost saving onto our customers making our pricing extremely competitive.

Even the most difficult and complex designs can be made by us, through a variety of materials and thicknesses. We can even bend metal sheets into shapes, if required.
