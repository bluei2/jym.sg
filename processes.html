Our Processes

1. Customer request delivery date.
2. Customer issue P.O. No. xxxx
3. JYM Job Order No. xxxx
4. Drawing distribution
        a) Programmer
        b) Production
5. Prepair material and accessories D/.O. No. xxxx
(Material Supply D.O. No.) M.S.,, Aluminun
6. Laser Cut and Bending QC check.
        a) Check the parts on the table.
        b) Check using correct equipment (Calliper, Angle Rule)
7. Welding QC check.
        a) Check parts on the table.
        b) Check using correct equipment (Angle Rule)
8. Powder coating QC check
        a) Check for physical and visible irregularity in conducting powder coating.
        b) Make sure all the surface are coated with powder coating.
9. Final QC checking/Assembly QC check.
        a) Check and confirm to the customer drawing for accuracy and ensure and customer's standards are followed.
        b) All the material and packaging are completely installed and properly packed.
10. Delivery D.O. Record
11. Reject items, Instruct workers to rework items if not complying.
12. NCR (Non Conformity Report) to be issued.
